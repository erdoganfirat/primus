                    ## Platforms

                    ### TVC
                    <p>We prefer an engaging language where we try to convey the brand and the deal within the first 5 seconds. We use an opening sentence starting with Hepsiburada and a packshot sentence ending with Hepsiburada.</p>

                    ### Radio
                    <p>We are progressing with radio spots parallel to the TVC. In the openings, we include texts with references to moments like heavy traffic when radio is widely listened to. In our DJ talks communications, we proceed with texts reminiscent of the DJ and radio listener's intimate conversation</p>

                    ### For CRM text (mailing - SMS - in-app)
                    <p>we aim to prompt user action using clickbait, energetic, and dynamic young texts. Incorporating widely used and beloved emojis, our goal is to engage users.</p>

                    ### Banner
                    <p>Kısa, net, anlaşılır mesajlarla ilk bakışta mesajımızı ve varsa indirimlerimizi vermeyi hedefliyoruz. Görsel dünyada metin kullanıldıysa banner metniyle tekrar etmemesine dikkat ediyoruz.</p>

                    ### CTA
                    <p>We aim to deliver our message and any discounts, if available, at a glance with short, clear, and understandable messages. In the visual aspect, we pay attention to not repeat the banner text if text is used.</p>

                    ### Social media
                    <p>In our content, instead of long videos, we provide concise information. On our Social Media platforms, we create more entertaining content tailored to the spirit of each platform. In our YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok content, we produce fun materials linked to our products using real-time trends and popular trends on Instagram at the moment</p>

                    ### Advertorial
                    <p>60 and 45-second models are available. It is crucial to align with the linguistic consistency of the campaign for whichever campaign is in question. For example, if we are organizing an advertorial specifically for 'Efsane Gençlik' we prepare our texts using keywords used by young people, creating a text that is young, dynamic, intimate, and feels like a mutual conversation with the youth</p>

                    ### Lower third
                    <p>We create lower third parallel to our TVC scripts. These texts will last for a maximum of 8 seconds and consist of 3 or 4 frames, with each frame containing a meaningful message on its own.</p>
