                    ## Usage
                    <ul className="list disc mb32">
                        <li>If not specified for the component, all radius must always have the same value to ensure the shape is not distorted.</li>
                        <li>The radius of the inside of the nested design elements is half of the radius of the outside.</li>
                        <li>When you want to design a circle, make the radius of the rectangle you draw 9999 px to get a circle.</li>
                        <li>The radius of the corners of two sticked design elements where they touch each other is 0 px.</li>
                    </ul>

                    <div className="row row-cols-2">
                        <div className="col">
                            <ul className="list disc mb24">
                                <li>The border radius of the exterior corner should be equal to the sum of the border radius of the Interior corner and the distance between the two elements.</li>
